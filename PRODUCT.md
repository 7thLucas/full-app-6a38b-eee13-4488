# XOGame — Product Overview

## What It Is
XOGame is a simple, clean 3x3 Tic Tac Toe web application for casual players. Two players take turns placing X and O on a 3x3 grid. First to get 3 in a row (horizontal, vertical, or diagonal) wins. Board resets after each game.

## Users
- Casual players
- Friends and family playing together on one device

## Core Feature
- Play a complete 3x3 Tic Tac Toe game
- Turn-based: Player X goes first, then Player O alternates
- Winner detection: check rows, columns, diagonals after each move
- Draw detection: when all 9 cells filled with no winner
- Board reset: after win or draw, reset button clears the board
- Status display: show whose turn it is, who won, or if it's a draw
